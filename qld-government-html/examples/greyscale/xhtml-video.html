Hi, and welcome to our Building Queensland website.

This site is a great way for you to find out what our record job-creating building program is delivering in your local community.

Whether you are in Cape York, Cloncurry, or Currumbin, building work is happening right now, right across Queensland.

We're building new hospitals and upgrading existing ones. We're building more roads, new police stations and better schools.

And we're working on projects that embrace Queensland's wonderful environment and lifestyle, with new children's playgrounds, walking tracks and swimming pools.
